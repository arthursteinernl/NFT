Collect your Digital Artwork

The boundaries between virtual and physical spaces are dissolving. We can stroll through virtual museums and collect digital art in the form of nun-fungible tokens (NFTs).

As part of the exhibition ‘Don’t Turn Around’ we have created a free limited edition of 25 digital artworks. The works can only be collected if you physically visit the exhibition. Once you have obtained your digital collectible, you are able to store it in your digital art collection or show it in a gallery in a decentralized virtual world such as Cryptovoxels and Decentraland.

How to collect your work

Step 1

Register here for your visit to the exhibition ‘Don’t Turn Around’ and indicate you would like to receive your digital artwork.

Step 2

Visit Haarlemmerstraat 2 where you will receive a card with a unique password. With this password you can get your artwork

Step 3

In order to get your digital artwork, you need to have a wallet which accepts the ethereum currency. We recommend the use of MetaMask. The installation instructions can be found here.

Step 4

Enter your password in the field below and connect it to your MetaMask. You will be asked to pay a small fee to the blockchain to make an ownership contract
