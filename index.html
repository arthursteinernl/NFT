Collect your Digital Artwork

In reaction to the global NFT hype, Studio Continuo presents the limited edition NFT, "Don't Turn Around" by Polina Kanis. As an experiment we have created a free limited edition of 25 digital artworks.

The works can only be collected if you physically visit the exhibition and ask for the secret password.

Through her work, Polina Kanis reflects on human action and agency in an age of rapid automation, financialization and algorithmic governance. How can we emancipate ourselves from the central financial infrastructures that rule our day-to-day life?

Once you have obtained your digital collectible, you are able to store it in your MetaMask Wallet or show it in a gallery in a decentralized virtual world such as Cryptovoxels and Decentraland.

How to collect your work

Step 1

Register for your visit to the exhibition ‘Don’t Turn Around’ and indicate you would like to receive the NFT.

Step 2

Visit Haarlemmerstraat 2 where you will receive a card with a unique password. With this password you can get your artwork

Step 3

In order to get your NFT, download MetaMask as an extension in your Chrome Browser on your computer. Our website ownly works on a desktop and not on a mobile. Download instructions can be found here

Step 4

This NFT is for free. But you need around 10 euro in Ethereum to pay for the transaction costs. If you already own Ethereum, you can transfer a small amount to your Metamask. If you don’t own Ethereum, you can purchase it through MetaMask for a minimum 10 EUR.

Step 5

Click Start on your computer and connect to your MetaMask. Enter your secret password and confirm in MetaMask. You will be asked to pay a small fee to the blockchain to make an ownership contract. This is an experiment. We appreciate your feedback. Please reach out to Arthur by email.

MetaMask is required
